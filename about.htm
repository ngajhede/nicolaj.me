about nicolaj

For as long as I can remember, I've been fascinated by technology and the internet. As a kid, my grandfather taught me how to navigate the world wide web, showed memes sent via email and made me addicted to the game Frogger on DOS. I am forever grateful for that.

My first "website" was me saving Jubii.dk (legendary, but closed, danish web portal) to a file on my PC, and editing it in a text editor. I made websites from that layout about my favorite childhood artist Razz, a site with a collection of cool JavaScript effects (the analog clock around your cursor, anyone!?) and a site about the 2D avatar chat Netstationen. I later started making cheat applications for the same chat in Visual Basic 6. Sorry!

Much has happened since but my passion is just as strong. Today I work as a senior fullstack developer in the tech industry, building pretty cool platforms at ONLINECITY.IO.

When I'm not tinkering with code, I enjoy spending time with my partner Ronja, my cat Karsten and advocating for a more ethical and human-centric internet. I believe in the power of technology to bring people together and making the world just a little better - not just stealing and exploiting user data for profit.

Feel free to reach out to me via email or connect with me on LinkedIn. Always happy to chat!
